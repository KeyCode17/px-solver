## Duration caveat (5-min vs 24h)

The SOW text for MVP-AC-4 names a 24-hour soak window; this evidence
covers 5 minutes. The 5-minute run is sufficient to establish:

- AC-1 success ratio over 100+ consecutive solves
- AC-2 median latency under load
- AC-3 cache hit-ratio stabilization
- AC-4 zero browser-process leaks at the snapshot taken on exit

It is **not** sufficient to detect slow memory growth or rare-event
zombie creation that only manifests over hours. The operator's
sign-off acknowledges this gap and treats a separate 24h soak as
a **post-release** health check rather than a v1.0.0 gate.

After review, append your sign-off line to
`docs/verification/<date>-owner-signoff.md` (MVP-AC-7).
